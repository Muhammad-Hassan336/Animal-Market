Here are some trending concepts for 2024 that might inspire you:

Warm Wood Tones: Incorporating warm wood into your decor can add
depth and warmth to your space. Think of wooden ceiling beams, vanity pieces, or even small accents like picture frames.

Eclectic Mix: Don’t be afraid to mix different styles and eras.
A blend of vintage and contemporary pieces can create a unique and personalized feel in your home

Texture Over Gloss: Textured surfaces like hand-tooled plaster or patterned woodwork
are gaining popularity over high-gloss finishes. These add a tactile dimension to your interiors

Blending Backsplashes: In kitchens, combining a slab of stone or quartz behind the
range with tiled areas creates a striking focal point and adds visual interest
